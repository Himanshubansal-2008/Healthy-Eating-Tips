Nutrition Basics

Healthy eating means getting the right balance of proteins, carbs, fats, vitamins, and minerals. Choose natural and fresh foods whenever possible.

Healthy Recipes

Grilled Chicken

A lean source of protein. Marinate chicken with olive oil, lemon, and herbs, then grill for a healthy, flavorful meal.

Fresh Salad

Mix lettuce, tomatoes, cucumbers, and olive oil dressing. Add seeds or grilled tofu for extra nutrition.

Fruit Smoothie

Blend banana, berries, yogurt, and honey for a refreshing and energy-packed smoothie.
